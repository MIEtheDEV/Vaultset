# Vaultset — User Guide

## What is Vaultset?

Vaultset is an all-in-one platform for trading card collectors. It lets you manage your card collection, track market values, buy and sell cards, and connect with the collector community.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Go to the Vaultset homepage and click **Start for Free**
2. Enter a username, email address, and password
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You will be signed in and redirected to your Dashboard

### Signing In

1. Click **Sign in** from the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

### Forgot Your Password?

1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link
4. Follow the link and enter a new password

## Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home base. It shows:

- **Total Cards** — total number of cards in your vault
- **Collection Value** — estimated total value (coming soon)
- **Active Listings** — cards and products you have listed for sale or trade
- **Pending Trades** — cards you have marked as available for trade

### Recently Added
A live feed of the last 6 cards added to your inventory.

### Watchlist
Cards from the marketplace you have saved to watch. Click any entry to view its full listing.

### Quick Actions
Shortcuts to common tasks — Add Card, Browse Market, Start a Trade (coming soon), View Profile (coming soon).

### Generate Report
Click **Generate Report** to produce a printable summary of your full collection.

## Inventory

Your inventory is split into two sections: **Cards** and **Products**.

### Adding a Card

1. Go to **Inventory** and click **Add Card**
2. Search for the card by name using the Pokémon card search
3. Select the correct card from the results
4. Fill in the details:
   - **Condition** — Near Mint, Lightly Played, etc.
   - **Finish** — derived automatically from the card variant
   - **Quantity** — how many copies you own
   - **Paid Price** — what you paid (for cost tracking)
   - **List Price** — your asking price if selling
   - **For Sale / For Trade** — toggle to list on the marketplace
   - **Grader / Grade** — if the card is professionally graded (PSA, BGS, CGC)
5. Click **Save**

### Editing a Card

From the Inventory grid, click the edit icon on any card to update its details.

### Removing a Card

From the edit page, use the **Remove** button to delete the card from your inventory.

### Sealed Products

Click **Products** from the Inventory page to manage sealed product purchases (booster boxes, ETBs, etc.). You can add, edit, link cards opened from a product, and mark products for sale or trade.

---

## Marketplace

The Marketplace shows all cards and sealed products listed for sale or trade by the community.

### Browsing Listings

- Use the grid to browse all available listings
- Click any card to view its full listing detail, including condition, grade, and asking price

### Watchlisting a Card

On any listing detail page, click the heart icon to add it to your Dashboard watchlist.

### Viewing a Seller's Listings

Click a seller's username to browse all of their active listings.

### Listing Your Own Cards

Mark a card as **For Sale** or **For Trade** when adding or editing it in your Inventory. It will automatically appear in the Marketplace.
